Compete. Log. Win together.

FitClub turns fitness into a team sport. Join a club, form or join a team, and compete in time-bound rounds. Every workout earns points for your team. Track your daily goals, weekly activity, and see your team rise on the leaderboard. The app supports both light and dark themes — screenshots below show the dark theme.

Your fitness. Your team. One app.

Everything you need to stay accountable and compete together.

Home

Your dashboard at a glance: current round with days left and points this week, your team name and rank, daily goal progress ring, workouts and streak, weekly activity grid, and your contribution to the team. One tap to the leaderboard or to log a workout.

Leaderboard

Switch between Teams and Individuals: see which teams are winning (top three in podium cards, then the full list with points) or which members are ahead. Your team is highlighted so you can track your rank and how many points you need to catch the leader. Round total and “pts behind #1” keep the competition clear.

Past round summary

Go back to all previous rounds and open any round to see how it finished. Each summary shows the winning team (with trophy and total pts), the round date range, and top contributors — individuals ranked by points with medals for the top three. Tap “View full leaderboard” to see full team and individual metrics and performance for that round.

Log workout

See your recent logged workouts at the top — then smart-select one with “Repeat” to log the same activity again or “Edit” to tweak it. Log new workouts by choosing activity type, duration (required), and optional distance or heart rate. Points are calculated using MET (metabolic equivalent) so intensity and duration determine how many points you earn. Today’s total and daily cap are shown so you know where you stand.

Team

See your current round, team rank, total points, and the full members list with each person’s points and % contribution. Members can view all of this and track their own contribution. Team leaders (and admins) can add members from the club and remove members from the team, so you can grow the squad or keep it focused. “You” is highlighted so you always see your place on the team.

Profile

Your identity (name, email) and workouts this round. My Clubs lists every club you’re in with your role (Member, Admin) and the active round; tap a club to switch context. Join club (invite code) or Create club to start a new one. Admins get Admin tools: create challenge rounds, manage members, manage teams, and edit club info. Profile settings are one tap away.
